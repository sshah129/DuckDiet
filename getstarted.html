Getting Starting with DuckDiet

To get started with DuckDiet, navigate to the Menu tab, where you can build your own dietary plan with our interactive site. Then:

  1. Set your Daily Calorie Goal
    * Important: You must set your calorie goal before proceeding!
  2. Find the items you are consuming under Menu Selection
  3. Enter each item and calorie count in their respective fields, and the current date under Add Items
  4. Submit and watch your calories!
  5. If you go over your calorie goal, you will recieve a warning. Try and stay under your goal!
